🚀 Getting Started

This software turns your webcam into a controller for your computer. You move your hand to move your mouse, scroll through pages, or change the volume. It uses a camera to watch your hand movements and translates them into actions. You do not need to write code to use this tool.

🖥️ System Requirements

  • ✅ Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
  • ✅ Hardware: A working webcam connected to your computer.
  • ✅ Memory: At least 4GB of RAM.
  • ✅ Lighting: A well-lit room for the camera to track gestures clearly.

  4. Open the file once the download finishes.

  6. Launch the program from your desktop shortcut.

ℹ️ How to Use Gestures

Once you open the software, the camera activates. Keep your hand visible within the camera frame for the software to track your movements.

  • Move Mode: Hold your hand up and move your index finger to drag the cursor.
  • Scroll Mode: Move your hand up or down to scroll through documents or web pages.
  • System Control: Use specific hand signals to adjust volume or screen brightness.

🛡️ Staying Safe

We prioritize your safety while using this tool. If the software makes an unexpected movement, press the 'Q' key on your keyboard. This acts as a global kill switch and stops the software immediately. If the camera loses sight of your hand, the application enters a paused state to prevent unwanted control. This ensures you always maintain total command over your system.

💡 Tips for Best Performance

For the smoothest experience, position yourself in a space with steady light. Avoid backlight or shadows that obscure your hand. Keep your movements controlled and deliberate. The software includes built-in smoothing, so you do not need to move your hand rapidly for the cursor to follow. If you encounter issues with tracking, check your camera connection or lens cleanliness.
